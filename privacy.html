EWelcome to BubuPod Inc. ("we," "our," or "us"). Protecting your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you visit or purchase from our website, www.bubupod.com. By using our services, you agree to the practices outlined here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, phone number, and shipping/billing addresses.
  • Payment Information: Securely processed through our payment gateway (not stored by us).
  • Order Data: Product details, preferences, and feedback related to your purchase.
  • Device and Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and website usage patterns.
  • Communication Records: Messages, emails, or inquiries sent to us.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Fulfill and deliver your orders efficiently.
  • Communicate updates, promotions, and support information.
  • Enhance our website and tailor your experience.
  • Comply with legal requirements and safeguard against fraud.

3. Sharing Your Information

Your information is not sold or rented. However, we may share it with:

  • Service Providers: Such as shipping companies, payment processors, or analytics providers.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or in response to valid legal requests.
  • Business Transfers: In case of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies help us provide a seamless experience by:

  • Remembering your preferences and improving site performance.
  • Tracking website usage for analytics and personalized content.

You can disable cookies via your browser settings, though some features may be limited.

5. Data Security

We employ robust security measures to protect your information. While we strive for complete security, no method is entirely foolproof. Use our website at your discretion.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: View the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request removal of your personal data.
  • Opt-Out: Decline marketing communications.
  • Portability: Receive a copy of your data in a portable format.

For assistance, contact us at support@bubupod.com.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may link to third-party services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their policies before sharing personal information.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. If we inadvertently collect data from a child, we will promptly delete it.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be available on this page. Continued use of our website signifies your acceptance of the updated terms.
